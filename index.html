About Mouka

For decades, Mouka has been the silent partner in millions of Nigerian homes, dedicated to the art and science of rest. We believe that a productive day begins the night before, which is why we pour our expertise into every fiber, foam, and fabric we produce.

Our approach is simple: We combine cutting-edge sleep technology with a deep understanding of human ergonomics. Whether you need the firm support of an orthopedic mattress or the plush embrace of our luxury pillows, our products are engineered to align with your lifestyle and body's specific needs. We don't just manufacture mattresses; we craft the sanctuary where you recharge, dream, and find peace.
